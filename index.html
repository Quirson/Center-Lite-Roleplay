Server Rules

Follow these guidelines to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players

1

Respect & Conduct

Be respectful to all players and staff members at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

2

No Cheating or Hacking

Cheating, hacking, or using any third-party modifications is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate permanent ban.

3

Bug Abuse Prevention

Bug abuse is not allowed. Report any bugs you find to staff immediately instead of exploiting them for personal gain.

4

Roleplay Standards

No DM (Deathmatch), RK (Revenge Kill), MG (Meta Gaming), or PG (Power Gaming). Maintain high roleplay standards at all times.

5

Language Policy

Use only English or Albanian in public chat. Private messages and faction chats may use other languages.

6

No Advertising

Advertising other servers, communities, or external services is strictly forbidden and will result in immediate punishment.

7

Staff Authority

Follow the staff's instructions at all times. Arguing with or disrespecting staff members will result in severe consequences.

Important Notice

Violation of any of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity. Staff decisions are final. For appeals or questions, contact us through our Discord server.
